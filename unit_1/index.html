Rebuilding Together Portland

Welcome to Rebuilding Together Portland!

For the past 26 years, Rebuilding Together Portland has been helping low-income homeowners in the City of Portland!

Rebuilding Together Portland is a nonprofit organization that assists low-income homeowners with needed home repairs at no cost to the homeowner. We help the elderly, people with disabilities, families with children, and Veterans throughout the City. Repairs span from carpentry, plumbing, and electrical, to painting, debris removal, and more. All work is completed by volunteers, both skilled and general. We have two programs, the National Rebuild Day and the Safe and Healthy Repair Program to help Portland homeowners!
